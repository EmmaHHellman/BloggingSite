A booming fashion trend

Active wear is becoming very popular. Women are buying sport's bras and stretchy capris. Even if they aren't excercising, women are still wearing activewear. It's a popular choice of clothing for all occasions.

Some popular active brands include Old Navy, Lululemon and Calia by Carrie Underwood
